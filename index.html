Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of physiotherapy services, including rehabilitation, sports therapy, and pain management. Our goal is to cater to all your physical health needs!

How can I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is easy! Just visit our website or call our friendly staff, and we’ll get you scheduled in no time!

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept various insurance plans. Check with us to see if we’re in-network with your provider!

Are internships paid?

Our internships offer valuable experience, and while they may not be paid, the knowledge and skills you gain are priceless!

What should I wear to therapy?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows for movement. We want you to feel free to stretch, bend, and conquer!
